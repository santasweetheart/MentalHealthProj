Mental Health

Worrying all the time? Is your mood getting in the way of your academic success? Our team of experts can help guide you toward mental and emotional health and stability.

Our Services

Joining a university community is all about adjustment–new schedules, new academic demands, new social pressures, new independence. There’s a lot to juggle.

Our Behavioral Medicine staff of psychiatrists, psychologists, clinical nurse specialists, licensed mental health clinicians, and clinical social workers is well versed in the many issues that can crop up over an academic career.

Find@Northeastern.
Mental health support when you need it anytime, anywhere.

You spoke and we listened. Introducing Find@Northeastern, your 24/7 immediate access connection to the mental health support and resources you need to feel better on the path ahead. With just one call, wherever you are in our community, youll reach someone to listen, respond, and be there to help.

New options and people to smooth the way

Find@Northeastern offers

  • Immediate support with a licensed mental health provider
  • Personal assistance in locating and scheduling the help you need
  • Five free counseling sessions each fall and spring semester
  • Telehealth options for wherever you are
  • Appointments with local therapists
  • Resources and tools to help you along the way

All full-time degree seeking students are eligible for Find@Northeastern
